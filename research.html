Inferring Copy Number States from Single-Cell Sequencing Data

T. Giorgadze • Preprint • 2025

single-cell copy number statistical genomics
Abstract

We present a framework for robust copy-number state inference in single-cell sequencing. The method combines probabilistic modeling with regularization across genomic segments and accounts for coverage heterogeneity, enabling accurate detection of subclonal CNAs.
